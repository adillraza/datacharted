Why Custom Connectors Matter

Not all platforms provide ready-made connectors. Many CRMs, call tracking systems, or niche SaaS tools don't integrate with your existing data stack.

The Problem

Standard connectors (Fivetran, Airbyte, Funnel.io) cover many popular tools — but they can't keep up with every niche platform or fast-changing API.

Without integration, valuable information gets stuck in silos — impossible to combine with the rest of your reporting.

The Solution

Custom connectors ensure:

  • Complete visibility – every platform contributes to your reporting
  • Flexibility – connectors adapt to your specific needs and business logic
  • Scalability – once built, connectors run automatically
